Carol Samantha Woodard Obituary; In Loving Memory of Carol Samantha

Saying farewell to someone we love and appreciate is often a challenging experience. On December 22, 2023, at the age of 53, Carol Samantha Woodard, who was born in Union, South Carolina, died away.

Her family and friends must now say their final goodbyes to their beloved Carol Samantha Woodard, who resided in Newberry, South Carolina. If you would like to express your condolences to the family, you can do so in the guestbook that has been provided. You may also send a lovely flower arrangement to the funeral service or light a candle in remembrance of Carol Samantha Woodard. Both of these options are available to you.

It was her father, Charles P. McClurkin, who passed away before she did that. Mother Sue Carol McClurkin, her husband Anthony Woodard, Sr., her children Anthony Woodard, Jr. of Newberry, South Carolina, Santana S. Woodard of Newberry, South Carolina, and Precious Shi-ann Woodard of Union, South Carolina, as well as her siblings Bridget McClurkin of Union, South Carolina, Annette Reeder of Charleston, South Carolina, and Bruce E. McClurkin of Union, South Carolina, are among those who are able to carry on her legacy. In addition, she is survived by three grandkids, a large number of aunts, uncles, nieces, nephews, six brothers-in-law, and a variety of other relatives and friends.

The Fred B. Pratt Memorial Chapel, located at 601 South Street, Newberry, South Carolina 29108, will be the location of the visitation that will take place on Wednesday, December 27th, 2023, from 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM. The Purity Baptist Church, located at 292 Amber Ln, Blair, South Carolina 29015, will be the location of a celebration of life that will take place on Thursday, December 28th, 2023 at 2:00 PM.
