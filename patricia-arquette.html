Patricia Arquette - Biography

Discover all you need to know about actress Patricia Arquette, from awards to her dazzling movie resume and more

    Patricia Arquette is an American actress. She was born in 1968 to Lewis Arquette, an actor, and Brenda Olivia 'Mardi' (née Nowak), who worked as a therapist. Her family lived on a commune in Bentonville, Virginia for a period of time. Patricia's entire family are performers, her siblings David, Rosanna, Alexis and Richmond.

    When she was 20, Patricia had a son named Enzo with her boyfriend Argentine musician Paul Rossi. In 1995, she wed Nicolas Cage – a union that lasted five years.
