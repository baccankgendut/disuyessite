David Castañeda

Birthday

October 24, 1989 (34)

David Castañeda is a Mexican-American actor who gained widespread recognition for his role as Diego Hargreeves in the Netflix series “The Umbrella Academy.”

Building a rich portfolio of roles in both film and television, Castañeda’s career has seen him perform across a variety of genres. He has acted in drama, action, and fantasy, taking roles in movies like “Sicario: Day of the Soldado” and TV series like “Jane the Virgin” and “Switched at Birth.” However, his role in “The Umbrella Academy” remains a cornerstone of his career.

Castañeda shared the screen with Elliot Page and Tom Hopper in “The Umbrella Academy,” Josh Brolin and Benicio del Toro in “Sicario: Day of the Soldado,” Gina Rodriguez and Justin Baldoni in “Jane the Virgin,” Vanessa Marano and Katie Leclerc in “Switched at Birth,” and Jesse Eisenberg in “We Have Always Lived in the Castle.” Other collaborations worth mentioning include Anthony Mackie in “Point Blank,” and Milo Ventimiglia in “The Path,” along with Kiefer Sutherland in “Designated Survivor” and Clive Standen in “Taken.”
