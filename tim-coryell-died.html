Who Was Tim Coryell?

Tim Coryell was a dedicated warrant officer at the Morgan County Sheriff’s Office in Indiana. He began his journey with the Sheriff’s Office as a reserve officer and later became the warrant officer in 2018. Tim’s commitment to serving his community was unwavering; he was available around the clock, 24/7. Whether you needed assistance locating someone, an additional officer for a specific task, or information about individuals in the county, Tim Coryell was the person to call. He went above and beyond his duty as a law enforcement officer; he was a steadfast pillar of strength and integrity in the community. Regardless of the time, day or night, Deputy Coryell was always there to help.

Throughout his years-long career, he touched the lives of numerous individuals, leaving behind a lasting legacy of safety and security that will be remembered by all who knew him.
