Madonna treats exposed Australian fan to Sydney concert

Last week Josephine Georgiou found herself at the centre of a media storm when she was exposed on stage by Madonna. A spokesperson for Josephine told the Daily Mail Australia: “"Three days after the show, Madonna flew Josephine and her mother to Sydney for the final show of her Australian tour on Sunday night.

Josephine Georgiou shared a photo of herself outside the Sydney concert

She went on to chat briefly to the teen and her mum before throwing her a bouquet of flowers.Josephine has previously spoken out about her headline-hitting encounter with Madonna in Brisbane, describing it as the "best moment of my life".

The 17-year-old later shared a picture of herself on the plane home, holding her bouquet from Madonna
