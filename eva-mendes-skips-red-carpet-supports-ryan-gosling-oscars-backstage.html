Eva Mendes skips red carpet to support Ryan Gosling at Oscars see intimate glimpse

The Barbie actor is nominated for Best Supporting Actor for playing Ken

    Eva Mendes may have stated early into awards season that she wasn't intending to hit up any red carpets with her longtime partner Ryan Gosling, but the support still remains.

    The actress, 50, took to her Instagram to reveal that she had eschewed the glitz and the glam of the 96th Academy Awards' red carpet to support her "man" behind the scenes.

    Eva revealed on Instagram that she was right beside Ryan, 43, backstage at the rehearsals for the Oscars which took place on Saturday, March 9, sharing peeks of her outfit while posing outside his dressing room as he prepared for his big night.

    The Canadian-American actor is nominated for Best Supporting Actor for his turn in the year's highest grossing movie, , playing the role of Ken, which has been described as "scene-stealing."

    The star will also perform his signature himbo power ballad "I'm Just Ken" at the ceremony, which is nominated for Best Original Song, along with all the other nominees (Billie Eilish's fellow track "What Was I Made For?" is the predicted winner in the category).
