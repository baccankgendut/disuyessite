Moon Knight Season 2 Release Date, Plot, Cast &amp; Characters

Jeremy Slater has developed a miniseries for Disney+ titled Moon Knight based on the Marvel Comics superhero of the same name. As the sixth TV show in the MCU, it follows in the same vein as the movie.

The story follows Marc Spector and Steven Grant, two aliases of a man with dissociative identity disorder (DID), as they become embroiled in a mystery involving the gods of ancient Egypt. Slater is in charge of writing, and Mohamed Diab is in order of directing.

We’ve researched when Season 2 of Moon Knight might be available, and it looks like Marc and Steven may be back for more Marvel action.

Moon Knight Season 2 Plot

Marc Spector’s adventures as Moon Knight would continue in the second season. Avatar Khonshu dealt with Harrow and his cultists in the first season, but many additional themes may be developed in a follow-up.

Many people probably want to see the masked hero take on Werewolf by Night, but we think it would be more interesting if the following series adopted the Shadow Knight plot. In this story, Randall, Marc’s brother, returned as the terrible Shadow Knight, a twisted version of Moon Knight who became a type of twisted avatar of Khonshu.

Marc’s third ego, the allegedly unscrupulous Jake Lockley, who shot Harrow (Ethan Hawkes), is similarly a loose end. We don’t think Marc or Steven know about him yet, but that probably won’t be the case for long.
