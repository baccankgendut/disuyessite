Who Is Jessie Js Boyfriend?Reveals Pregnancy And Babys Gender

Fans of the British pop star Jessie J have been speculating about the identity of her boyfriend, who they believe to be Chanan Safir Colman, a basketball player currently playing for the Israeli club Hapoel Haifa. 

According to his Instagram profile bio, Colman has been a professional athlete for 16 years and is also the founder of Camp Colman, which is based in Seattle, Washington.

In February 2023, Jessie J attended The BRITs for the first time in a decade and revealed to Capital’s Rio Fredrika on the red carpet that she was expecting a baby boy just one month after announcing her pregnancy. 

When asked about baby names, the singer replied that she was still thinking about it and expressed her excitement about seeing her baby’s face.

Jessie J looked stunning in a red outfit as she proudly displayed her baby bump at the event, similar to how Rihanna showcased her pregnancy during the Super Bowl halftime show.

The singer also shared a music video on her Instagram page, in which she sang to her unborn child. In the caption, she revealed that every time she sings a particular song, her baby moves like crazy, leading her to believe that it’s the perfect song for him. 

She also confirmed that she is expecting a boy. Many of Jessie J’s fans have expressed their excitement and joy over the news of her pregnancy, eagerly anticipating updates on the journey of the soon-to-be mother.

But who is Chanan Safir Colman, Jessie J’s boyfriend?

Chanan Safir Colman, a Danish-Israeli professional basketball player who played for an Israeli club, was born in 1984 in Gladsaxe Municipality, Denmark. He has been an active athlete for 16 years and continues to play basketball.

Last year, an online news magazine published images of Chanan and pop star Jessie J kissing in Los Angeles during a date. Later, Jessie J confirmed their relationship, which she had kept hidden.

In May of that year, the singer expressed her excitement about being in a true love relationship with Chanan but didn’t reveal much else about their romance.

In September of the same year, Chanan and Jessie J were spotted in Rio de Janeiro, enjoying a sunny getaway before her performance at the 2022 Rock in Rio Festival.
