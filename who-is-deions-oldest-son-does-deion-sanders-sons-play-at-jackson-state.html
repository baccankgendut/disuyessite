Who is Deions oldest son? Does Deion Sanders sons play at Jackson State?

Deion Luwynn Sanders Sr. “Prime Time” or “Neon Deion” is an American former professional football player and athlete, sports analyst, and football coach. Deion is currently the head football coach at Jackson State University.

Deion Sanders has been married twice and both have not been successful. He married Carolyn Chambers in 1989 and the relationship ended in 1998. The couple had two children. He then married Pilar Biggers-Sanders (1999–2013), with whom he has three children.

Who is Deion’s oldest son?

Deion’s oldest son is Deion Sanders Jr., who played his college football at SMU. He lined up as a wide receiver, catching 69 passes for a total of 1,499 yards during three seasons with the Mustangs.

Do Deion Sanders’ sons play at Jackson State?

Three of his boys seem to follow the footstep of their father. Shilo Sanders was reported to have transferred to Jackson State from the University of South Carolina in 2020 to play with his brother Shedeur Sanders.
