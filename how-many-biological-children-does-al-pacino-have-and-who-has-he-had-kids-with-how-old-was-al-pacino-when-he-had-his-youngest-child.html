Al Pacino is an American actor who has never been married but has been in a romantic relationship with a number of women.

He is a father and this article will dive into his parental life to find out the number of biological children he has and who they are as well as the mothers of his children.

How many biological children does Al Pacino have, and who has he had kids with?

Pacino, according to the information father is the biological father of four children – two boys and two girls including twins.

He is the father of Julie Marie Pacino, Anton James Pacino, Olivia Pacino and Roman Pacino.

Julie is his first child and he welcomed her with his ex-girlfriend, acting coach Jan Tarrant. Anton James Pacino and Olivia Pacino are his twin children and he welcomed them with actress Beverly D’Angelo.

Roman Pacino is his last child and son and he welcomed him with producer, Noor Alfallah.

How old was Al Pacino when he had his youngest child?

Among the four children of the actor, Roman Pacino happens to be his youngest. He was born on June 15, 2023. At the time, the actor was 83 years old making him one of the oldest fathers on record.
