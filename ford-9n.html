Ford 9N Tractor Price, Specs, Reviews, Horsepower, Weight, Engine Oil Capacity, Implements, History & Pictures

The Ford N-series tractors were a line of farm tractors manufactured by the Ford motor company between 1939 and 1952, spanning the 2N, 8N, and 9N models.

The Ford 9N was the first American-made production-model tractor to join Harry Ferguson’s three-point hitch system, a design still used on most modern tractors today. It was announced in October 1939.
