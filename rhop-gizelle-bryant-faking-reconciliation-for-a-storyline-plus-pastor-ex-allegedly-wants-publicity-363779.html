RHOP: Gizelle Bryant Faking Reconciliation For A Storyline? Plus Pastor Ex Allegedly Wants Publicity

Gizelle Bryant and Jamal Bryan have rekindled their romance. According to new reports, it may be for her season five Real Housewives of Potomac storyline.

In addition to needing a ruse to interest Bravo fans, her ex-husband who is the pastor of a megachurch is going along with it in order to gain more publicity.

Recently, the Baptist place of worship has been visited by the likes of T.I and Kanye West who performed his world-famous Sunday Service, the chance to be on reality TV will attract even more visitors.

Meanwhile, a source is claiming that this ‘fateful’ reconciliation is not what it seems.

The insider told Mediatakeout: ‘[Gizelle] desperately needed a new storyline after her ex-boyfriend (retired ex NBA player) Sherman Douglas dumped her for good . . . and no other men are interested in her so she had no other options but [her ex] Jamal.’

They went on to claim that Gizelle is even paying him a portion of her salary to be on the show.
