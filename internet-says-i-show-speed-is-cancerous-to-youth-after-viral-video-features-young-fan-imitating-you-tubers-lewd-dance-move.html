Internet says IShowSpeed is 'cancerous to youth' after viral video features young fan imitating YouT

CINCINNATI, OHIO: Renowned YouTube streamer Darren Watkins, known as IShowSpeed, held his inaugural live stream in an IRL (In Real Life) setting on November 5, 2023, in Manchester.

A large crowd of fans gathered to see this special event. As expected, the streamer interacted with many of his supporters, including a young boy who unexpectedly imitated the YouTuber's inappropriate but signature dance move that mimicks the act of fellatio.
